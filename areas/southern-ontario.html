Southern Ontario

The Work That Brings Us Out

ED Industrial is based in Milton. We work closest to home for day to day mechanical support, but the work that brings us further out is the kind that matters most to get right.

If your facility is further from Milton, the conversation changes. Shift coverage and quick call ins are not the pitch. What makes sense at distance is a contractor who can mobilize a full crew, coordinate a complete scope, and deliver the mechanical work your plant needs without you managing five different contractors to get it done. That is where we come in.

How It Works

One Contact. Full Scope.

You contact us with your scope. Our project managers review it and put together a plan built around your facility, your schedule, and what the job actually needs. If the mechanical scope requires outside trades, electrical, concrete, controls, gas, structural, HVAC, we coordinate them. You deal with one contact through the whole job.

We have coordinated large projects for clients across Southern Ontario in sectors where the mechanical scope was too complex or too critical to hand to a contractor who was not fully equipped for it. A 20,000 sq ft fabrication shop, CWB and TSSA certified tradespeople, engineers and AutoCAD designers in house, and project managers who build the plan before the crew leaves Milton. That is what we bring to a job regardless of where it is.

If your plant is anywhere in Southern Ontario and you are planning a shutdown, a new line, a relocation, or a project that needs a dependable all in mechanical contractor, reach out. We will figure out what the job needs and build a plan around it.
