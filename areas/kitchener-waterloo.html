Kitchener-Waterloo's Industrial Landscape

Manufacturing in Kitchener-Waterloo, Ontario

Kitchener-Waterloo has a deep manufacturing base that goes well beyond the tech sector the region is known for. The industrial zones along Bleams Road, Shirley Avenue, and the Highway 8 corridor in Kitchener house plastics, rubber, automotive parts, metal fabrication, and food processing operations. Waterloo's manufacturing is concentrated along Northfield Drive and the Erb Street corridor.

The region's legacy in rubber and tire manufacturing. Uniroyal and BF Goodrich both operated here. That legacy evolved into a diverse manufacturing economy. Plants in KW run everything from precision machining to heavy production, and the skilled-trades workforce here is one of the strongest in Ontario thanks to Conestoga College and the region's apprenticeship tradition.
