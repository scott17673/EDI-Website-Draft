The GTA's Industrial Landscape

Manufacturing in the Greater Toronto Area

The Greater Toronto Area is one of the largest manufacturing regions in North America. From Oshawa in the east to Hamilton in the west, and north through York Region to Barrie, the GTA houses thousands of industrial operations. Manufacturing corridors along the 401, 403, 407, and QEW connect food processing plants in Mississauga to automotive suppliers in Brampton, steel operations in Hamilton, and packaging facilities in Scarborough.

The diversity is staggering. Food and beverage, pharmaceutical, automotive, plastics, chemical processing, recycling, building products, aggregate, and general manufacturing all operate within an hour's drive of each other. This is the market ED Industrial was built to serve.
