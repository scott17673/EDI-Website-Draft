Millwrighting

Industrial Millwrighting for Manufacturing Facilities

Full-scope millwrighting for plants across the GTA and Southern Ontario. We handle equipment installs, line modifications, conveyor work, rotating equipment, planned maintenance programs, shutdown turnarounds, and outage support. Our 433A Red Seal certified millwrights work out of our Milton shop with field crews dispatched across the region. Whether it is a single machinery swap, a precision laser alignment, or a full plant turnaround, the crew that shows up knows the scope before they walk through the door.

  • Equipment and machine installs, maintenance, and modifications
  • Rigging, installs, demolition, and relocation
  • Conveyor work, rotating equipment, bearing and shaft replacement
  • Planned maintenance programs, shutdown turnarounds, and outage support
  • Laser alignment, precision leveling, and equipment calibration
  • On-site and in-shop capability
