Fabrication & Welding

CWB Certified Fabrication From Our Milton Shop

Structural steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and custom fabrication from our 20,000 sq ft shop in Milton. We design it, build it, load it on a flatbed, and install it in the field. Mezzanines, platforms, guarding, hoppers, chutes, conveyor structures, custom equipment frames, and modified production components. Fit-up and prep work happen in the shop before anything reaches your site, which cuts field time and reduces disruption to your operation. CWB certified welders handle both structural and process applications in-shop and on-site.

  • Structural steel, stainless steel, and aluminum fabrication
  • Mezzanines, platforms, guarding, hoppers, and chutes
  • New production line components and modified equipment
  • Fit-up, prep work, and field-ready delivery
  • Flatbed delivery and field installation
  • On-site welding for plant scopes and shutdown work

Materials We Work With

  • Aluminum 3003, 5052 and 6061-T6
  • Carbon Steel A36/44W, A516Gr 50, 60 and 70, QT100/A514, AR 200, 400 and 450
  • Austenitic Stainless Steel 304 and 316
  • Duplex (ferritic-austenitic) 2205 Stainless Steel (UNS S32205 S31803)
  • High-Performance Super-Austenitic Stainless Steel AL-6XN (UNS N08367)
