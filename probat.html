Built for Food Processing.

ED Industrial is part of the PROBAT Group, one of the world's leading manufacturers of food processing machinery. PROBAT builds the machines that process coffee, cocoa, nuts, popcorn, and a wide range of other food products. Their equipment runs in food processing facilities across North America and around the world.

That connection puts ED Industrial in a different position than a standard mechanical contractor. We work alongside state of the art food processing machinery every day. That exposure raises the bar for how our crews approach food processing facilities, the standards we hold ourselves to, and the knowledge we bring to every scope in this sector. We are only getting sharper.

What It Means for Your Facility

We support food processing operations of all kinds, from incoming raw material handling through roasting, grinding, coating, packaging, and everything in between. Sanitary piping, process piping, equipment installs, line modifications, conveyor systems, structural platforms and guarding, planned maintenance, and shutdown support are the scopes we run most in this sector.

The Machinery PROBAT Covers

Coffee roasters and processing lines, cocoa processing equipment, nut roasters and handling systems, popcorn and snack food processing, bulk material handling, grinding and milling systems, and custom food processing machinery across a wide range of applications.

What We Do in Food Processing Facilities

Sanitary and process piping to food-grade standards, equipment installs and commissioning, line modifications and upgrades, planned maintenance programs, shutdown support, conveyor systems and bulk handling, structural platforms and mezzanines, guarding, on-site welding, rigging and machinery moves, and full project coordination when a scope involves multiple trades.
