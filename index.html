Who We Are

ED Industrial

ED Industrial is a Milton-based industrial mechanical contractor. We handle millwrighting, fabrication, process piping, and installation work for manufacturing plants across the GTA and Southern Ontario.

We're part of the PROBAT Group, one of the world's leading manufacturers of coffee roasting and processing equipment. That connection runs deep in food and beverage and gives our customers access to OEM-level support that most mechanical contractors can't offer.

The team is built around 433A Red Seal millwrights, pipefitters, welders, and engineers, all in house. The Milton shop handles fabrication, fit-up, and project prep. Field crews handle the rest.

One contact. Prepared crews. Real answers.

Services

What We Do

01

Millwrighting & Maintenance

Conveyor work, rotating equipment, bearing and shaft replacement, planned maintenance programs, and shutdown preparation.

02

Fabrication & Welding

Platforms, guards, frames, and custom assemblies built in-shop. Structural and architectural welding. Fit-up, prep work, and field-ready delivery.

03

Process & Stainless Piping

Process piping and stainless systems for food, beverage, and chemical plants. Tie-ins, modifications, upgrades, and full new-scope installs.

04

Shutdown & Install Support

Coordinated shutdown execution from outage planning through mechanical installation. Scope management, field crew dispatch, and shop support.

05

Rigging & Heavy Lifting

Field rigging for equipment setting, removal, and repositioning. Coordinated with mechanical crews for full-scope install work.

06

Mechanical Installations

Equipment setting, alignment, anchoring, and tie-in work for new and replacement process equipment across industrial facilities.

07

Conveyor Systems

Design support, fabrication, installation, and maintenance for belt, screw, drag, and bucket conveyor systems.

08

Project Management

Scope development, scheduling, field coordination, and execution management for multi-trade industrial projects.
